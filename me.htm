About this site

This website is a restored version of my original homepage from the late 1990s.
For some reason — perhaps instinctively — I saved the entire site on a CD nearly 30 years ago.
Recently I rediscovered it and decided to bring it back online, more or less as it once was.

It is presented as a small digital time capsule from the early days of the web —
when pages blinked, counters mattered, and everything felt a little experimental.

Please note that some links may be outdated or temporarily broken.
I update and repair things when I have the time and inspiration to do so.
This restoration is an ongoing project and also a nostalgic revisit to another era.
